AI Token



What is an AI Token:

Tokens are pieces of text, which can be as small as a single character or as large as a whole word. For example, the word "chatbot" is one token, while the sentence "The AI is smart." consists of five tokens: "The", "AI", "is", "smart", ".".
‍
‍
Each LLM like GPT4o has a cost per token.
‍
- GPT 4o charges $5 per 1 Million INPUT tokens.
- GPT 3.5 charges $0.5 per 1 Million INPUT tokens.
‍
Output tokens also have their own cost.
‍
- GPT4o charges $15 per 1 Million OUTPUT tokens.
‍
The more information you are sending to the LLM and the more information the LLM is sending back to you, the more it costs.
