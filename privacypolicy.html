Privacy Policy

Welcome to Folderr.com

‍
Owned and operated by Folderr LLC in the United States, our privacy policy aims to clarify how we handle your Personally Identifiable Information (PII) online. PII, as recognized by US privacy law, is information that can uniquely identify, contact, or locate an individual.

Collection of Information

‍
Personal Information (PII):

We collect PII, such as names, email addresses, and financial information, only when you voluntarily provide it. This may occur when you purchase services, submit inquiries, or engage with our site's features.
‍

Non-Personal Information (Non-PII):

Automatically collected via cookies and IP addresses, Non-PII includes browser type, settings, and site usage details, helping us tailor content to visitor preferences.

‍

Use of Your Information
Your PII is used strictly for the purposes it was collected for, including:
- Responding to your queries and providing site access.
- Fraud investigation and policy enforcement.
- Processing transactions and improving our services.
- Sharing with affiliated entities for site maintenance and promotional purposes, under strict conditions to prevent further use.
- Internal auditing, data analysis, and research.

We also outline specific scenarios such as legal compliance, emergency situations, and with your explicit consent.

‍

Managing Your Information

‍
You control your PII. You can update or correct your information by contacting us directly. Your PII may also be used for direct marketing, research, compliance, and safety purposes, always with the option to opt-out.

‍

Cookies and Security

‍
Our cookie policy details our use of cookies for tracking and advertising. We implement security measures to protect your PII, although the internet's inherent risks mean we cannot guarantee absolute security.

‍

California Residents (CCPA)

‍
We respect CCPA guidelines, offering rights to information, access, and deletion of your PII. We do not sell personal information. Requests for information or deletion can be made via email.

Children's Privacy

‍
Our services are not intended for children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect their information. Compliance with COPPA is a priority.

‍

Third-party Websites

‍
We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites linked to ours. Review their policies as they may differ from ours.

‍

Data Retention

‍
Your information is retained as necessary for service provision, compliance, and legal obligations.

‍

Changes to the Policy

‍
We may update this policy and will notify users of significant changes. Continued use of the site after updates implies acceptance.

‍

Acceptance of This Policy

‍
Using this site signifies your acceptance of our privacy policy. If you disagree, please refrain from using our services.

Contact Us

For questions or concerns regarding this policy, please contact us at hello@folderr.com.
